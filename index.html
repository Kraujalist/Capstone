Climate Exposure Health Tool

Predicting neighborhood-level health vulnerability to extreme heat up to 2075. Developed in partnership with the Ministry of Health.

Health Vulnerability Score (0-100): Higher = greater risk. Combines seven factors:

  • Heat (+4.17): PET temperature impact
  • Elderly (+3.44): % aged 65+
  • Income (+2.98): % low-income residents
  • Children (+2.89): % aged 0-14
  • Old Buildings (+0.66): % built before 2010
  • Stress (+0.66): High stress index

Linear Regression Model: Trained on 11,554 neighborhoods
Vulnerability = -1047.90 + 4.46×Heat + 3.25×Income% + 1.43×Elderly% + 1.40×Rental%

Scoring: Raw scores normalized to 0-100 scale for visualization
Data: CBS (demographic), KlimaatAtlas (heat), GGD (health)
